Mostafa Hadavand, PhD

I did my PhD in Geostatistics in university of Alberta. Geostatistics is a branch of applied statistics with focus on probabilistic study of spatial phenomena. Currently, I work as a data scientist at Silver Creek Software in Enterprise Data Warehouse (EDW) project for Alberta Environment and Parks (AEP). Also, I work as a research associate and geostatistician at Centre for Computational Geostatistics (CCG), University of Alberta.

Feel free to look around my website. I have posted some information about my past and current research and also some of my Publications.
