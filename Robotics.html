Robotics and Mechanical Design

Prior to joining Centre for Computational Geostatistics (CCG) , my work was focused on robotics and developing surgical tools. I hold a MESc in biomedical engineering from the university of Western Ontario. During my work at CSTAR (Canadian surgical technologies and advanced robotics), I developed a surgical robotic system for lung cancer diagnosis and treatment.
